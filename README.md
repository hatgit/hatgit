## Mastering Meta-Prompting (Mid-2023) 


<img src="https://github.com/hatgit/hatgit/assets/5213035/ce15c67c-7a98-475b-b8b6-d230190b4388" width="15%" height="auto">

In mid-2023, I coined the term **meta-prompting** and wrote one of the earliest books on the concept: [Mastering Meta-Prompting: The Key to AI-Driven Success](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0C6PRFNL5). The idea: use AI to iteratively design and refine prompts, shifting the human role from operator to architect of intent.
